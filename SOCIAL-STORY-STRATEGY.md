## 🎯 STORY TYPES THAT WORK (Ranked)

### 1. 🔥 **TEASER CLIP** (5-10s scene cut)
**Best for:** Driving curiosity → click through
**Format:**
- Take 1 scene from latest EP (high-action or emotional peak)
- Add text overlay: episode + cliffhanger hook
- CTA: "Watch full episode → link"

### 2. 💬 **QUOTE GRAPHIC** (iconic dialog)
**Best for:** Emotional connection + shareability
**Format:**
- Static image with dialog quote
- Character face in background
- Episode reference

### 3. 📊 **POLL/QUESTION STICKER**
**Best for:** Algorithm engagement + retention
**Format:**
- Direct question with 2-4 options
- About character preferences or predictions

### 4. ⏰ **COUNTDOWN STICKER**
**Best for:** Pre-upload anticipation
**Format:**
- Set countdown to upload time
- Hype copy + thumbnail teaser

### 5. 🎬 **BTS (Behind-the-Scenes)**
**Best for:** Building creator authority
**Format:**
- CapCut timeline screenshot
- "Making EP X" with process snippets
- Tools used reveal

### 6. 🖼️ **CHARACTER SPOTLIGHT**
**Best for:** New character debut hype
**Format:**
- Character portrait with personality card
- Backstory teaser

### 7. 🎭 **MEME / REACTION**
**Best for:** Viral organic reach
**Format:**
- Screenshot iconic face moments
- Caption with current trend

## 📱 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### **Instagram Stories** ⭐ (PRIMARY)
- **Stickers:** Poll, Question, Countdown, Music, Link, Hashtag (use all!)
- **Add #WrongMecha hashtag sticker** every story = searchable
- **Add Location sticker** "Aeden" or "Mecha World" for fun discoverability
- **Music sticker** with epic mecha soundtrack = bonus reach
- **Link sticker** to YouTube video (use sparingly — 1-2 per day)
- **Repost from feed:** any new post → share to story

### **Facebook Stories**
- **Auto-share from IG** if connected (efficient)
- **Add caption with hashtags**
- **Tag any mecha anime pages**
- Less effective than IG but zero extra effort if cross-posted

### **TikTok Stories** (less critical)
- TikTok algorithm = Reels/regular posts > Stories
- Use TikTok Stories for:
  - Quick "EP X live" announcement (1 per upload)
  - Reply to comments via story
- Better to focus TikTok energy on **Reels posts** (clips of best scenes)

## 🏆 GROWTH HACK

**Cross-platform funnel:**
```
TikTok Reel (best clip)
   ↓
Caption: "Full EP on YouTube"
   ↓
TikTok bio link → YouTube channel
   ↓
Story repost from TikTok to IG
   ↓
IG bio link → YouTube
   ↓
YouTube views ↑↑↑
```

**Most viral platform:** TikTok > IG Reels > YT Shorts (for new accounts)
**Conversion:** TikTok views = good for awareness, YouTube = long-form retention + monetization

**Strategy:** Build presence on TikTok first → funnel to YouTube.
